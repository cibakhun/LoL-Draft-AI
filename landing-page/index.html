No Fluff. Just Math.

DraftDiff is unapologetically technical. We built an offline engine that ingests massive amounts of Match-V5 data directly into local SQL databases for rigorous training.

Monte Carlo Tree Search

Calculating the optimal draft isn't a simple heuristic. DraftDiff uses extensive MCTS permutations to traverse potential pick/ban states and identify win-condition nodes against specific opponent compositions.

PyTorch Synergy Models

Evaluating champion performance requires multi-dimensional analysis. Our deep neural networks ingest spatial lane mappings, patch metadata, and cross-role synergies to output precise drafting deficits.

Gold Curve Ingestion

Win rates lie. Item spikes don't. We rip timeline endpoints directly from Riot API to calculate differential xp/gold states at 15 minutes, mapping actual champion influence over game states.
